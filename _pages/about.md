I am Akshay, a Final-year Bachelors student majoring in Mechanical
Engineering and Minoring in AI at the [Indian Institute of Technology Madras (IITM)](https://iitm.ac.in) and an incoming PhD Student at [Massachusetts Institute of Technology](https://mit.edu/) in the CCSE-MechE joint programme. 

**Research Interests**: Vast majority of computational power in the world is used to understand, design and optimize complex scientific and engineering systems like cars, airplanes etc. My research goal is to use AI to accelerate and democratize this computation to (1) achieve similar accuracy with much less compute, (2) solve problems that were computationally impossible, and (3) democratize the usage of these tools by reducing the knowledge barrier required for it's effective usage. 

At IIT Madras, I was advised by [Prof. Balaji Srinivasan (WSAI, IITM)](https://wsai.iitm.ac.in/faculty/balaji-srinivasan/) on building Physics-Informed Extreme Learning Machines (PI-ELMs), a fast, intrepretable and sustainable alternative to Physics-Informed Neural Networks. Previous to this, I have worked on introducing physics constraints into deep learning models for inverse airfoil design under guidance of [Prof. Nagabhushana Rao Vadlamani](https://home.iitm.ac.in/nrv/index.html) and Prof. Bharath Govindarajan. I was also fortunate to have worked under the mentorship of [Prof. Balaraman Ravindran](https://dsai.iitm.ac.in/~ravi/) and [Dr. Gokul S Krishnan](https://cerai.iitm.ac.in/people/gokul-s-krishnan/) at [Center for Responsible AI (CERAI)](https://cerai.iitm.ac.in/) in building Robust Bias Evaluation Frameworks.
