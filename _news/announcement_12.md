🚀 Exciting Update: Wrapping up at Fujitsu Research! 🚀

I am thrilled to share that I have successfully completed my research internship with the Next Gen AI Lab at Fujitsu Research!

During my time here, I had the incredible opportunity to work on developing a Training-Free Memory Layer for Vision Language Action (VLA) Models. As embodied AI continues to advance, finding computationally efficient, training-free architectural enhancements to help VLA models retain and utilize memory over long-horizon tasks was an incredibly rewarding technical challenge to tackle.

This journey wouldn't have been the same without the phenomenal support and collaborative culture of the team.
