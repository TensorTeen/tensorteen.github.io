Honored to receive two awards at #AAAI2026 for our research on #llm bias #evaluation.
As AI scales globally, we must ensure it doesn't leave the #GlobalSouth behind. By developing more #inclusive bias benchmarks, we are working to empower these regions with AI tools that are fair, representative, and safe for everyone.
Kudos to my colleague Santhosh G S and my mentors Dr. Gokul S Krishnan, Prof. Balaraman Ravindran and Prof. Sriraam Natarajan from Centre For Responsible AI (CeRAI) at Indian Institute of Technology, Madras for making this possible!!
