I am honored to share that I’ve been selected for the Kiran Award 2024 – “The Best Academic Student” by the Indian Society for Non-destructive Testing (ISNT), Chennai Chapter! This recognition, instituted by Prof. D.R. Kiran, celebrates leadership qualities and knowledge in non-destructive testing (NDT).

A heartfelt thanks to my advisor, Prof. Prabhu Rajagopal (IIT Madras), for his unwavering guidance, support, and mentorship throughout my academic journey. My gratitude also extends to ISNT for this prestigious honor and for fostering excellence in the NDT community.
